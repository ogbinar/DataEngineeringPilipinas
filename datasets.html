Philippine Data Sources

Government, Public Administration, and Census Data

  • Philippine Standard Geographic Code (PSGC) - Comprehensive list of administrative divisions in the Philippines.
  • COVID Data via DOH Data Drop - Official Department of Health repository for COVID-19 data.
  • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) - Various statistical datasets provided by the Philippine Statistics Authority.
  • PSA Annual Poverty Indicators Survey (API) - Annual data on poverty indicators in the Philippines.
  • PSA Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES) - Information on family incomes and expenditures in the Philippines.
  • Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index (CMCI) - Data assessing the competitiveness of Philippine cities and municipalities.
  • BSP Banks Directory - Directory of banks and financial institutions operating in the Philippines.
  • BSP Financial Service Access Points - Listing of financial service access points across the Philippines.
  • Unified Accounts Code Structure (UACS) - Standard for government accounting in the Philippines.
  • Open Data Philippines (beta) - Official portal for various government datasets in the Philippines.
  • World Bank Philippines Data - Economic data and indicators for the Philippines from the World Bank.
  • Awesome Data Philippines by BNHR - Curated collection of Philippine data resources.
  • Philippines - Subnational Administrative Boundaries - Data on administrative boundaries within the Philippines.

Environmental, Geographic, and Natural Resources Data

  • HydroSheds Data - Hydrological data and high-resolution mapping.
  • Earthquake Data - Seismic activity data from the United States Geological Survey.
  • Hazard Data - Data from the World Environment Situation Room.
  • Natural Resources Data - Datasets related to the Earth’s natural resources.
  • Typhoon Data - Tracking and information on typhoons affecting the Philippines.
  • Climate Data - Climate datasets from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA).
  • Land Cover by ESRI - Worldwide data on land cover from Esri.
  • Marine Boundaries - Detailed information on global marine regions.
  • Coral Reefs - Data and mapping of coral reefs.

Economic and Social Data

  • Economic and Social Database - Economic and social data provided by the Philippine Institute for Development Studies.

General and Multi-Purpose Data Sources

  • Google Datasets - Explore datasets offered by Google Cloud.
  • Google Research Datasets - Search engine for research datasets.
  • BigQuery Public Datasets - Access to various public datasets through Google BigQuery.
  • Public API Lists for Data Engineering - List of public APIs useful for data engineering projects.
  • GADM Data - Detailed maps and data of administrative areas of countries, useful for GIS and mapping projects.

Health and Life Sciences Data

  • World Health Organization (WHO) Collections - Global health data from the World Health Organization.
  • Healthcare Images - Public datasets of healthcare images.
  • Genome Datasets - Data from the International Genome project.

Climate and Earth Sciences Data

  • NOAA Data Products - Datasets from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
  • Climate Data - Comprehensive climate datasets.

Miscellaneous and Other Data Resources

  • Unicef Data Collections - Data on global child welfare from UNICEF.
  • US Population Data - Population statistics from the US Bureau of Labor Statistics.
  • Stanford Open Policing Project - Data on police traffic stops in the United States.
  • Scikit-Learn Datasets - Datasets available in the Scikit-Learn library for machine learning.
  • PyTorch Datasets - Datasets available in the PyTorch library for machine learning.
  • Huggingface Datasets - Hub for various machine learning datasets.
  • Wikipedia ML Research Datasets - List of datasets for machine learning research.
  • OECD - Data from the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development.
  • Datahub - Free and open instance for hosting and sharing datasets.
  • Kaggle - Platform for data science competitions, hosting, publishing, and analyzing data.
  • data.world - Social network for hosting and sharing data.
  • Academic Torrents - BitTorrent-based platform for sharing large datasets in the academic community.
